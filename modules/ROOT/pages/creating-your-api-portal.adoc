The process of creating your API portal includes these steps:

. xref:connecting-to-salesforce.adoc[Connect to Salesforce]
+
Connect to Salesforce and link your Anypoint organization to your Salesforce organization.
. xref:styling-your-api-portal.adoc[Set Up the API Portal]
+
Use the guided setup to set up the portal and apply styling options to brand your portal. After the setup and styling are complete, the portal is created. 

== Before You Begin
Before creating your portal, we recommend that you have the following information:

* Review the xref:prerequisites.adoc[Prerequisites]
* Image for company logo
* Color scheme for styling your portal
* Image to use as a header for your portal
