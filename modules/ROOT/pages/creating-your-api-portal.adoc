The process of creating your API portal includes these steps:

. xref:connecting-to-salesforce.adoc[Connect to Salesforce]
+
Connect to Salesforce and link your Anypoint organization to your Salesforce organization.
. xref:styling-your-api-portal.adoc[Style Your API Portal]
+
Use the guided design to style and brand your API portal. After your style options are saved, the portal is created. 

== Before You Begin
Before managing your portal, you need the following:

* Review xref:prerequisites.adoc[Prerequisites]
* APIs in Exchange
* Image for company logo
* Color scheme for styling your portal
* Image to use as a header for your portal
