Users can sign up to get access to an API portal when self-registration is enabled by the portal administrator.

The following is an example portal:

image::aeh-portal-login.png[Portal login]

The signup process includes the following steps:

. Enter the URL of the portal in a browser window.
. Enter the first name, last name, and email address.
. Click *Sign Up*.
+
All self-registers are automatically approved. Ensure that the link to the portal is only accessible to trusted parties. 
. From the portal, click *Log in* and enter your username and password.
