= Managing Your Portal 
ifndef::env-site,env-github[]
include::_attributes.adoc[]

After your portal is styled using the guided setup, the Manage Your API Portal page provides options to manage APIs, users, and settings.

* xref:api-management.adoc[API Management]
+ 
Add your APIs from Exchange into your API portal, remove APIs from your portal, and view list of APIs that you have in Exchange.

* xref:user-management.adoc[User Management]
+ 
View and manage members, and pending requests.

* xref:settings.adoc[Settings]
+ 
Publish the portal, view the portal's site URL, and deactive or delete the portal.
