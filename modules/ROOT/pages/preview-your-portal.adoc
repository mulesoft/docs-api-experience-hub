= Preview Your Portal 
ifndef::env-site,env-github[]
include::_attributes.adoc[]
endif::[]

After making changes to your portal, you can preview the new version to ensure that it meets your expectations from the Manage your API pages or from the Builder:

. From the *Manage your portal* pages, click *Preview and publish your portal*.
. Verify that your changes are applied and displayed correctly. 
+
APIs that are visible only to administrators appear in this preview.
. To publish the portal, click *Publish*.
. To make changes, click *Back to Builder*.
