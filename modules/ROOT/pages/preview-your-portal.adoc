After you make custom changes to your Salesforce Experience Cloud site, you can preview the new version to ensure that it meets your expectations:

. Click *Builder*.
. Click *Preview*.
. Verify that your changes are applied and displayed correctly. 
+
APIs that are visible only to administrators appear in this preview.
. Click *Back to Builder* to continue to make changes.
